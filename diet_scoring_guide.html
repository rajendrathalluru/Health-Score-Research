Diet Scoring Guide

Current ThriveScore diet scoring logic with examples.

Each diet category is scored as:

  • 1.0 = best alignment
  • 0.5 = partial alignment
  • 0.0 = low alignment

1. Plant-Based Foods

This is the average of 3 sub-scores:

plant_based_score = average(fruit_veg, pulses, whole_grains)

Fruits and vegetables (q29)

Example:
Fruit/veg = 1.0, Pulses = 0.5, Whole grains = 0.5
plant_based_score = (1.0 + 0.5 + 0.5) / 3 = 0.67
