WCRF/AICR Cancer Prevention Score Calculator

Privacy Policy Effective: February 19, 2026

HealthScore Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data when you use our WCRF/AICR Cancer Prevention Score Calculator application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Health & Fitness Data (via Fitbit): Physical activity, steps, weight, BMI, and sleep data synced through the Fitbit API with your explicit consent.
  • Dietary Information: Food logs, meal entries, and nutritional data you manually enter or import.
  • Body Measurements: Waist circumference, BMI, and other metrics you provide to calculate your WCRF/AICR score.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, page visits, and feature usage for improving the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Calculating your daily WCRF/AICR Cancer Prevention Score.
  • Displaying your health dashboard, trends, and progress over time.
  • Syncing data with your connected Fitbit account.
  • Providing personalized recommendations to improve your score.
  • Maintaining and improving the application.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Fitbit Data

When you connect your Fitbit account, we access data via the official Fitbit Web API using OAuth 2.0. We only request the minimum scopes necessary (activity, weight, profile). You can revoke access at any time from your Fitbit account settings at fitbit.com/settings/applications.

Fitbit data is stored securely and used only to compute your WCRF/AICR score and display your dashboard.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and never stored in plain text. We use JWT-based authentication and HTTPS to protect data in transit.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for Fitbit data access at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Fitbit / Google Privacy Policy
  • USDA FoodData Central (read-only nutrition data)

8. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
